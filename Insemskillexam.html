Chocolate Cake

Delicious and easy homemade chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and milk.
  4. Pour batter into pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
